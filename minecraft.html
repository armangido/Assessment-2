Review

Minecraft is a sandbox game that allows players to build and explore infinite worlds. Its simple mechanics and endless creativity have made it one of the most beloved games worldwide, beloved by kids, countless hours and sleepless nights have been spent building, crafting and exploring only to die by the hands of a creeper.
